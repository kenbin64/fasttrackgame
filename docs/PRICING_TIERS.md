# Pricing Tiers

Last updated: 2026-03-06 • Version 1.0

## Overview
Initial pricing reflects a free introduction period. Pricing and benefits are subject to change with notice.

## Tiers (Subject to Change)
- Free Intro: Limited-time promotional access to select premium games and features; no guarantees of continued access.
- Member: Access to premium games (FastTrack, Breakbreaker3D), standard matchmaking, seasonal events.
- Pro: Everything in Member plus queue priority, expanded storage, early access features.
- Guild/Enterprise: Organization features, governance tools, premium support, volume options.

## Payments and Renewals
- Pricing, taxes, and billing periods will be presented at checkout when enabled.
- Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled in accordance with the Terms of Service.

## Changes and Notice
- We may update pricing and features. Material changes will be communicated in advance where required.
