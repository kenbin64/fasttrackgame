# Medallion Status

Last updated: 2026-03-06 • Version 1.0

## Overview
Medallion Status is a recognition and access program that rewards community participation, gameplay mastery, and contributions. Status may grant cosmetic badges, early feature access, queue priority, and guild privileges.

## Tiers
- Bronze: New members; baseline benefits.
- Silver: Established participation; occasional early access.
- Gold: High participation or skill; priority access and cosmetic rewards.
- Platinum: Top contributors; extended privileges.
- Founders (invite-only): Legacy recognition; special badge.

## Earning and Maintaining Status
- Gameplay: Achievements, fair play, and leaderboard placement.
- Community: Helpful forum activity, content sharing, mentoring.
- Contributions: Bug reports, UX feedback, localization, accessibility.
- Integrity: No cheating, harassment, or policy violations.
- Decay: Status can decay after prolonged inactivity; reminders are sent.

## Benefits Matrix (examples)
- Badge + profile highlight in chat and lobbies
- Early/preview access to new maps/modes
- Queue priority for premium servers during peak hours
- Guild creation/voting privileges at higher tiers
- Cosmetic unlocks and seasonal challenges

## Review and Appeals
- Status changes are automated with periodic manual review to ensure fairness.
- You may appeal downgrades or submit supporting evidence via support.

## Transparency
Aggregated statistics about medallion distribution may be published to maintain fairness and community trust.
