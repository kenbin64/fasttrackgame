# Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026-03-06 • Version 1.0

## Summary
We collect only what we need to operate and improve the Services, secure accounts, and comply with the law. We do not sell your personal data.

## Data We Collect
- Account identifiers: email, display name, device identifiers (hashed), IP (approximate).
- Authentication: one-time tokens (hashed), session identifiers.
- Gameplay: scores, achievements, preferences.
- Telemetry: performance metrics, feature usage (aggregated, pseudonymized where possible).
- Communications: support messages, feedback forms.

## How We Use Data
- Provide, secure, and improve Services
- Authenticate and prevent fraud/abuse
- Personalize gameplay and content
- Comply with legal obligations

## Legal Bases (where applicable)
- Contract (providing Services)
- Legitimate interests (security, improvement)
- Consent (marketing communications, optional analytics)

## Sharing
- Service providers under contract (e.g., hosting, email delivery), bound to confidentiality and data protection.
- Legal or safety requirements when necessary.
- We do not sell personal data.

## Retention
We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account subject to legal/operational limits.

## Your Rights
- Access, correction, deletion, portability (where applicable)
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Object to or restrict certain processing

## Cookies and Similar Technologies
We use strictly necessary cookies and, with consent, analytics cookies. You can control cookies in your browser.

## Security
We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards including encryption in transit, hardened sessions, rate limiting, and least-privilege access.

## Children
The Services are not directed to children under 13. If we learn a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it.

## International Transfers
Data may be processed in the United States or other countries with appropriate safeguards.
