# Guild Rules

Last updated: 2026-03-06 • Version 1.0

## Purpose
Guilds are player-led communities. These rules protect a welcoming, safe, and creative environment while supporting competitive play.

## Code of Conduct
- Be respectful. No harassment, hate speech, or targeted abuse.
- Play fair. No cheating, exploiting, or unauthorized automation.
- Keep it appropriate. No NSFW content in public spaces.
- Protect privacy. Do not dox or share private information.
- Follow the law. No illegal content or activity.

## Content Policy
- Original content only or content used with permission.
- Mark spoilers and sensitive content.
- Credit creators; respect licenses.

## Guild Administration
- Leaders must maintain an active moderator presence.
- Publish clear rules and escalation paths in the guild profile.
- Enforce consistently and document actions where feasible.

## Enforcement Ladder
1. Informal warning
2. Formal warning
3. Temporary mute/suspension
4. Kick/ban from guild
5. Platform-level escalation for severe violations

## Reporting and Appeals
- Report violations via in-app tools or support email with evidence.
- Moderation actions can be appealed; decisions reviewed by a neutral moderator or staff.

## Integration with Medallion Status
- Higher medallion tiers may unlock guild creation, governance voting, and moderation tools.
- Severe policy violations may lower medallion status.

## Changes
Rules may be updated for clarity or safety. Continued participation indicates acceptance of updates.
