What is Dimensional Computing?

Dimensional computing is a paradigm shift in how we think about data and objects. Instead of building structures piece by piece, we recognize that wholes already contain their parts ‚Äî just like in physical reality.

When you buy a car, you don't assemble the engine, pistons, and spark plugs yourself. They already exist within the car. You simply invoke them when needed ‚Äî open the hood to see the engine, remove a spark plug to inspect it.

ButterflyFX brings this natural model to computing. Objects aren't constructed from parts; parts are revealed from wholes. Data isn't stored in tables; it exists as coordinates in a dimensional substrate.

"The shape IS the data. The position IS the value. Every object contains infinite dimensions of detail ‚Äî we simply invoke what we need."
